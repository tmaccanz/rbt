Tan Tips
Pre Tan
Do not get a spray tan if you are peeling!
Please shave/wax/shower/exfoliate at least 3 hours before your tan! Your skin needs to be dry & your pores closed to ensure the best development of your tan.
Exfoliate! This is the most important tip as you are removing all of your almost dead skin cells and creating a fresh canvas for your tan to work with (avoid oil based exfoliants).
Use minimal soap prior to your tanning.
Do not apply moisturisers, deodorant or wash your hair immediately before tanning as the residue acts as a barrier (wipes are available to wipe underarms if you need to).
Remember to bring loose fitting clothing to put on after your tan.
Post Tan
We will discuss your post tan procedure with you during your appointment.
Avoid any water until your first rinse off shower (careful washing your hands as any drop will damage your tan)!
Quick rinse off until water runs clear.
Always pat dry after every shower.
No moisturiser after 1st shower.
Use a PH balanced body wash from day two of your tan when you can now shower as normal.
To keep your tan looking even & to ensure it lasts as long as possible it is recommended to moisturise daily with PH balanced moisturisers (for best results use a tan extender available for sale in salon) avoid thick creams & Shea butter.
Chlorine in pools/spas will strip your tan as chlorine is a bleach.
Please don't hesitate to phone or message us with any queries and most of all enjoy being a bronzed goddess!
